### Sublime插件-增强篇

---


- [WordCount](https://github.com/titoBouzout/WordCount)可以实时显示当前文件的字数
![](http://img.mukewang.com/56c41f560001e06d01630019.jpg)

- [EncodingHelper](https://github.com/titoBouzout/EncodingHelper)编码众多，比较丰富
![](http://img.mukewang.com/56c41f800001a87710990536.jpg)
安装后，在右下角的UTT-8右键可以将其他编码转换成UTF-8

- [ConvertToUTF8](https://github.com/seanliang/ConvertToUTF8)比上面的那个要方便，直接在菜单栏中可以转了，专为中文设计，妈妈再也不通担心中文乱码问题了
![](http://img.mukewang.com/56c41f980001f3e706000422.jpg)

- [Terminal](http://wbond.net/sublime_packages/terminal)Sublime版的在当前文件夹内打开
![](http://img.mukewang.com/56c41fa8000169cb03090228.jpg)

- [Side​Bar​Enhancements](https://github.com/titoBouzout/SideBarEnhancements)右键一下子多处那么多选择
![](http://img.mukewang.com/56c41fbd0001725b03440665.jpg)

- [SideBarFolders](https://github.com/titoBouzout/SideBarFolders)打开的文件夹都太多了，再用这个来管理文件夹
![](http://img.mukewang.com/56c41fd800011fbe04150186.jpg)

- [TrailingSpace](https://github.com/SublimeText/TrailingSpaces)强迫症患者必备
![](http://img.mukewang.com/56c420970001e9d801350035.jpg)

- [Search Stack Overflow](https://github.com/ericmartel/Sublime-Text-2-Stackoverflow-Plugin)就是不想动鼠标，直接在Sublime中打开浏览器搜索Stackoverflow
![](http://img.mukewang.com/56c420f7000181a404500111.jpg)
